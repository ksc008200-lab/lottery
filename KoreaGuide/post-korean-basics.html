Korean for Beginners — 50 Essential Phrases to Know Before You Go

March 25, 2026 · 8 min read

You don't need to be fluent to get around Korea. Even knowing a handful of phrases will earn you smiles, better service, and a much richer travel experience. Here are the 50 most useful Korean phrases — with simple pronunciation guides.

Pronunciation tip: In romanization below, "eo" sounds like the "u" in "but", "eu" sounds like the "i" in "sir", and "ae" sounds like "eh". Most sounds are close to English — just say it confidently!

Greetings & Basic Courtesy

KoreanRomanizationMeaning
안녕하세요
Annyeonghaseyo		Hello (formal, standard)
안녕
Annyeong		Hi / Bye (informal, friends)
감사합니다
Gamsahamnida		Thank you (formal)
고마워요
Gomawoyo		Thank you (casual)
죄송합니다
Joesonghamnida		I'm sorry (formal)
괜찮아요
Gwaenchanayo		It's okay / No problem
네
Ne		Yes
아니요
Aniyo		No
잠깐만요
Jamkkanmanyo		One moment please
모르겠어요
Moreugeseoyo		I don't know

Ordering Food & At Restaurants

KoreanRomanizationMeaning
이거 주세요
Igeo juseyo		This one, please (point at menu)
얼마예요?
Eolmayeyo?		How much is it?
맛있어요!
Massisseoyo!		It's delicious!
물 주세요
Mul juseyo		Water, please
계산해 주세요
Gyesan-hae juseyo		The bill, please
하나 더 주세요
Hana deo juseyo		One more, please
맵지 않게 해주세요
Maepji ange haejuseyo		Not spicy, please
포장해 주세요
Pojang-hae juseyo		To go, please
저기요!
Jeogiyo!		Excuse me! (calling a server)
카드 돼요?
Kadeu dwaeyo?		Do you take card?

Shopping

KoreanRomanizationMeaning
이거 있어요?
Igeo isseoyo?		Do you have this?
더 싸게 해주세요
Deo ssage haejuseyo		Can you give me a discount?
이거 얼마예요?
Igeo eolmayeyo?		How much is this?
너무 비싸요
Neomu bissayo		Too expensive
그냥 볼게요
Geunyang bolgeyo		Just looking (politely declining help)
다른 색 있어요?
Dareun saek isseoyo?		Do you have other colors?

Emergencies & Getting Help

Useful Everyday Phrases

KoreanRomanizationMeaning
한국어 조금 해요
Hangugeo jogeum haeyo		I speak a little Korean
천천히 말해주세요
Cheoncheonhi malhaejuseyo		Please speak slowly
다시 말해주세요
Dasi malhaejuseyo		Please say that again
이름이 뭐예요?
Ireumi mwoyeyo?		What's your name?
제 이름은 ___이에요
Je ireumeun ___ ieyo		My name is ___
반가워요
Bangawoyo		Nice to meet you
안녕히 계세요
Annyeonghi gyeseyo		Goodbye (said to person staying)
안녕히 가세요
Annyeonghi gaseyo		Goodbye (said to person leaving)
