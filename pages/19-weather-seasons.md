# Ch19: Weather & Seasons — 날씨 & 계절

<div class="tip">
      💡 <strong>Weather is the #1 conversation starter!</strong><br>
      Koreans talk about weather constantly — it's the perfect small talk topic. Korea has 4 beautifully distinct seasons: cherry blossoms in spring 🌸, hot humid summers ☀️, breathtaking autumn foliage 🍁, and cold snowy winters ❄️. Learning weather vocabulary opens up endless conversations!
    </div>

    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">📅 Months of the Year · 월</h3>
    <div class="card" style="background:#f0f7ff; border-left:4px solid #1A4A8A; margin:12px 0;">
      <div class="card-title">🔧 Easy Rule — Just add 월 (wol) = month!</div>
      <p>1월 (il-wol) = January &nbsp;|&nbsp; 2월 (i-wol) = February &nbsp;|&nbsp; 3월 (sam-wol) = March<br>
      Exception: 6월 = 유월 (yu-wol) · 10월 = 시월 (si-wol) — these drop the ㄱ/ㅂ!</p>

    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">💬 Weather Conversations · 날씨 대화</h3>
    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:20px; margin:14px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:10px; color:#1A4A8A;">☁️ Morning Small Talk · 아침 대화</div>
      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">오늘 날씨 어때요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(o-neul nal-ssi eo-ttae-yo?) — How's the weather today?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">아직 흐린데 나중에 비가 온대요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(a-jik heu-rin-de na-jung-e bi-ga on-dae-yo.) — Still cloudy, but they say it'll rain later.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">진짜요? 우산 가져가야겠다!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(jin-jja-yo? u-san ga-jyeo-ga-ya-gess-da!) — Really? I should take an umbrella!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">맞아요. 요즘 날씨가 너무 변덕스러워요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(maj-a-yo. yo-jeum nal-ssi-ga neo-mu byeon-deok-seu-reo-wo-yo.) — Right. The weather has been so unpredictable lately.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">그래도 가을이 오니까 선선해서 좋아요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(geu-rae-do ga-eu-ri o-ni-kka seon-seon-hae-seo jo-a-yo!) — Still, with autumn coming, it's nice and cool!</span></p>
    </div>
