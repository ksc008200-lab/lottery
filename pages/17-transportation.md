    <h3 style="margin-top:32px;">💬 Asking for Directions · 길 물어보기 대화</h3>
    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:22px; margin:14px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:12px; color:#1a5c2a;">🗺️ Finding Gyeongbokgung Palace</div>
      <p><strong>You:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">저기요, 경복궁이 어디예요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(jeo-gi-yo, gyeong-bok-gung-i eo-di-ye-yo?) — Excuse me, where is Gyeongbokgung?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>Local:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">여기서 멀어요. 지하철 타세요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(yeo-gi-seo meol-eo-yo. ji-ha-cheol ta-se-yo.) — It's far from here. Take the subway.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>You:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">몇 호선이에요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(myeot ho-seon-i-e-yo?) — Which line is it?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>Local:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">3호선 타고 경복궁역에서 내리세요. 5번 출구로 나오면 돼요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(sam-ho-seon ta-go gyeong-bok-gung-yeok-e-seo nae-ri-se-yo. o-beon chul-gu-ro na-o-myeon dwae-yo.) — Take Line 3 and get off at Gyeongbokgung Station. Come out exit 5.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>You:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">얼마나 걸려요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(eol-ma-na geol-lyeo-yo?) — How long does it take?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>Local:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">약 20분이요. 어렵지 않아요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(yak i-sip bun-i-yo. eo-ryeop-ji an-a-yo!) — About 20 minutes. It's not difficult!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>You:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">감사합니다! 정말 친절하세요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(gam-sa-ham-ni-da! jeong-mal chin-jeol-ha-se-yo!) — Thank you! You're so kind!</span></p>
