    <!-- AT THE HOSPITAL DIALOG -->
    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">💬 At the Hospital · 병원 대화</h3>
    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:22px; margin:14px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:12px; color:#C0392B;">🏥 Doctor's Visit · 진료</div>
      <p><strong>의사:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">어디가 불편하세요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(eo-di-ga bul-pyeon-ha-se-yo?) — Where are you uncomfortable? / What's wrong?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>환자:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">어제부터 목이 아프고 열이 나요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(eo-je-bu-teo mok-i a-peu-go yeol-i na-yo.) — Since yesterday my throat hurts and I have a fever.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>의사:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">기침도 해요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(gi-chim-do hae-yo?) — Are you coughing too?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>환자:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">네, 조금요. 그리고 온몸이 쑤셔요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(ne, jo-geum-yo. geu-ri-go on-mom-i ssu-syeo-yo.) — Yes, a little. And my whole body aches.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>의사:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">감기 같네요. 약 드릴게요. 푹 쉬세요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(gam-gi gat-ne-yo. yak deu-ril-ge-yo. puk swi-se-yo!) — Looks like a cold. I'll give you medicine. Please rest well!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>환자:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">감사합니다. 약은 어떻게 먹어요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(gam-sa-ham-ni-da. yak-eun eo-tteo-ke meog-eo-yo?) — Thank you. How do I take the medicine?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>의사:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">하루 세 번, 식후에 드세요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(ha-ru se beon, sik-hu-e deu-se-yo.) — Three times a day, after meals.</span></p>
    </div>
