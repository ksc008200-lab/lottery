    <h3 style="margin-top:24px;">💬 Travel Dialog · 여행 대화</h3>
    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:22px; margin:14px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:12px; color:#1A4A8A;">🗺️ Planning a Trip · 여행 계획</div>
      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">이번 주말에 뭐 해요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(i-beon ju-mal-e mwo hae-yo?) — What are you doing this weekend?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">부산 여행 가려고요! 해운대 가보고 싶어요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(bu-san yeo-haeng ga-ryeo-go-yo! hae-un-dae ga-bo-go sip-eo-yo.) — I'm planning to travel to Busan! I want to visit Haeundae beach.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">좋겠다! KTX 타고 가요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(jo-kess-da! KTX ta-go ga-yo?) — Sounds great! Are you taking the KTX?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">네, KTX로 2시간 반이면 돼요. 호텔도 예약했어요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(ne, KTX-ro du-si-gan ban-i-myeon dwae-yo. ho-tel-do ye-yak-haess-eo-yo!) — Yes, it takes 2.5 hours by KTX. I already booked the hotel!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">꼭 자갈치 시장에서 회 먹어봐요! 진짜 맛있어요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(kkok ja-gal-chi si-jang-e-seo hoe meog-eo-bwa-yo! jin-jja mas-iss-eo-yo!) — Make sure to eat raw fish at Jagalchi Market! It's really delicious!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">와, 추천 감사해요! 즐거운 여행 되길 바라요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(wa, chu-cheon gam-sa-hae-yo! jeul-geo-un yeo-haeng doe-gil ba-ra-yo!) — Wow, thanks for the recommendation! Hope you have a great trip!</span></p>
    </div>
