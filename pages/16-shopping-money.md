      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:12px; color:#1A4A8A;">🧥 Clothing Store · 옷가게</div>
      <p><strong>직원:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">어서오세요! 뭐 찾으세요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(eo-seo-o-se-yo! mwo cha-jeu-se-yo?) — Welcome! What are you looking for?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>손님:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">이 재킷 얼마예요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(i jae-kit eol-ma-ye-yo?) — How much is this jacket?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>직원:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">오만 원이에요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(o-man won-i-e-yo.) — It's 50,000 won.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>손님:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">너무 비싸요. 좀 깎아 주세요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(neo-mu bi-ssa-yo. jom kka-kka ju-se-yo!) — Too expensive. Please give me a discount!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>직원:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">사만 오천 원에 드릴게요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(sa-man o-cheon won-e deu-ril-ge-yo!) — I'll give it to you for 45,000 won!</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>손님:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">입어봐도 돼요? 다른 색깔 있어요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(ib-eo-bwa-do dwae-yo? da-reun saek-kkal iss-eo-yo?) — Can I try it on? Do you have other colors?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>직원:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">네! 파란색이랑 검은색 있어요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(ne! pa-ran-saek-i-rang geom-eun-saek iss-eo-yo.) — Yes! We have blue and black.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>손님:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">검은색으로 살게요. 카드 돼요?</span><br>
