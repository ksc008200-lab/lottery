🇰🇷 Why Korean Nouns Work Differently

교착어 Agglutinative
Nouns attach particles (조사) to show grammar — *밥을* (rice-OBJ), *밥이* (rice-SUBJ)

SOV Word Order
Subject + Object + **Verb last** — the noun comes before the verb, always

높임말 Honorifics
Some nouns have honorific forms — *밥* → *진지*, *이름* → *성함*
