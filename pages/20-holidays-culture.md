# Ch20: Holidays & Culture — 명절 & 문화

<div class="tip">

From the deep reverence of Seollal to the playful sweetness of Pepero Day, every celebration tells you something profound about what Koreans value: family, gratitude, community — and a good excuse to eat amazing food! 🎊

    <!-- TWO BIG TRADITIONAL HOLIDAYS -->
    <h3>The Two Great Traditional Holidays · 2대 명절</h3>
    <div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:20px; margin:20px 0;">

      <div class="card" style="border-top:5px solid #C0392B; padding:22px;">
        <div style="font-size:48px; text-align:center; margin-bottom:10px;">🌕</div>
        <div style="text-align:center; margin-bottom:12px;">
          <div class="hangul-cell" style="font-size:28px; font-weight:700;">설날</div>
          <div style="font-size:14px; color:#666; margin-top:4px;">Seollal · seol-lal</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700; font-size:16px; margin-top:4px;">Lunar New Year</div>
        </div>
        <ul style="padding-left:18px; line-height:2; font-size:14px;">
          <li>Lunar calendar: 1st day of 1st month (Jan/Feb)</li>
          <li>Family gathers · wear 한복 (hanbok)</li>
          <li>Bow deeply to elders → get money! 세뱃돈</li>
          <li>Eat 떡국 (rice cake soup) to gain a year</li>
          <li>Play 윷놀이 (yutnori) board game</li>
          <li>3-day national holiday</li>
        </ul>
        <div class="hangul-cell" style="display:block; text-align:center; font-size:16px; margin-top:12px; color:#C0392B;">새해 복 많이 받으세요!</div>
        <div style="text-align:center; font-size:12px; color:#888;">sae-hae bok man-i ba-deu-se-yo — Happy New Year!</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card" style="border-top:5px solid #E8C84A; padding:22px;">
        <div style="font-size:48px; text-align:center; margin-bottom:10px;">🌾</div>
        <div style="text-align:center; margin-bottom:12px;">
          <div class="hangul-cell" style="font-size:28px; font-weight:700;">추석</div>
          <div style="font-size:14px; color:#666; margin-top:4px;">Chuseok · chu-seok</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700; font-size:16px; margin-top:4px;">Korean Thanksgiving / Harvest Festival</div>
        </div>
        <ul style="padding-left:18px; line-height:2; font-size:14px;">
          <li>Lunar calendar: 15th day of 8th month (Sep/Oct)</li>
          <li>Give thanks for the harvest</li>
          <li>Ancestral memorial rites: 차례 (charye)</li>
          <li>Eat 송편 (songpyeon — half-moon rice cakes)</li>
          <li>Watch the full moon 달맞이</li>
          <li>3-day national holiday · biggest travel day!</li>
        </ul>
        <div class="hangul-cell" style="display:block; text-align:center; font-size:16px; margin-top:12px; color:#d4850a;">즐거운 추석 되세요!</div>
        <div style="text-align:center; font-size:12px; color:#888;">jeul-geo-un chu-seok doe-se-yo — Have a joyful Chuseok!</div>
      </div>
    </div>

    <!-- KOREAN CULTURAL CONCEPTS -->
    <h3 style="margin-top:32px;">🧠 Must-Know Korean Cultural Concepts · 꼭 알아야 할 한국 문화</h3>
    <div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:16px; margin:16px 0;">

      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #C0392B;">
        <div class="card-title">😤 눈치 (nun-chi)</div>
        <p>The ability to read the room — sensing others' feelings and the situation without being told. High 눈치 = socially intelligent. Low 눈치 = oblivious.</p>
        <p style="margin-top:8px;"><em>"눈치 없어!" (nun-chi eobs-eo!) — You have no social awareness!</em></p>
      </div>

      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #1A4A8A;">
        <div class="card-title">🤝 정 (jeong)</div>
        <p>Deep emotional bond — affection that builds over time between people. It's more than love or friendship. You feel 정 for people you've shared experiences with for a long time.</p>
        <p style="margin-top:8px;"><em>"정이 들었어요." (jeong-i deu-reoss-eo-yo.) — I've grown attached.</em></p>
      </div>

      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #27ae60;">
        <div class="card-title">😊 빨리빨리 (ppal-li ppal-li)</div>
        <p>"Hurry hurry!" — Korea's famous speed culture. Everything is fast: delivery in 1 hour, internet fastest in the world, same-day service everywhere!</p>
        <p style="margin-top:8px;"><em>"빨리빨리!" — Faster! Hurry up!</em></p>
      </div>

      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #E8C84A;">
        <div class="card-title">🍻 회식 (hoe-sik)</div>
        <p>Company/team dinner — a Korean workplace tradition where colleagues eat and drink together after work. Refusing can be awkward. Very important for team bonding!</p>
        <p style="margin-top:8px;"><em>"오늘 회식 있어요." (o-neul hoe-sik iss-eo-yo.) — We have team dinner tonight.</em></p>
      </div>

      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #7d3c98;">
        <div class="card-title">🎮 PC방 (pi-si-bang)</div>
        <p>PC café — a cultural institution! Koreans go to PC방 to play online games, watch content, and hang out. Open 24/7, fast computers, food available inside.</p>
        <p style="margin-top:8px;"><em>"PC방 갈래요?" (pi-si-bang gal-lae-yo?) — Want to go to a PC café?</em></p>
      </div>

      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #2e86c1;">
        <div class="card-title">🛁 찜질방 (jjim-jil-bang)</div>
        <p>Korean sauna/spa — open 24 hours, with hot rooms, cold rooms, sleeping areas, and food. Families go together. The perfect place after a long day!</p>
        <p style="margin-top:8px;"><em>"찜질방 가고 싶어요!" — I want to go to a jjimjilbang!</em></p>
      </div>
    </div>
