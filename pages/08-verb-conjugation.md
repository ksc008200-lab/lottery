# Ch08: Verb Conjugation — 동사 활용

<div class="card green">
      <div class="card-title">✅ Great News! Korean Verbs NEVER change by person!</div>
      <p>나는 먹어요 / 당신은 먹어요 / 그는 먹어요 — ALL THE SAME!</p>
      <p class="kr">나/당신/그 누가 말해도 동사 형태는 동일합니다!</p>
    </div>

    <h3>The Magic Formula · 마법 공식</h3>
    <div class="card">
      <div class="card-title">📌 Polite Present Tense (해요체)</div>
      <p>Remove <strong>다</strong> from dictionary form → get the stem → add <strong>아요</strong> or <strong>어요</strong></p>
      <p>✅ Stem ends in <strong>ㅏ or ㅗ</strong> → add <strong>아요 (a-yo)</strong></p>
      <p>✅ Everything else → add <strong>어요 (eo-yo)</strong></p>
      <p>✅ <strong>하다 (ha-da)</strong> → always becomes <strong>해요 (hae-yo)</strong></p>
    </div>
