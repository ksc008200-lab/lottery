    <h3 style="margin-top:32px;">🇰🇷 Korean Food Culture Tips · 한국 음식 문화</h3>
    <div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:16px; margin:16px 0;">
      <div class="card" style="border-top:3px solid #C0392B;">
        <div class="card-title">🥢 반찬 (ban-chan) — Side Dishes</div>
        <p>Korean meals always come with FREE side dishes called 반찬. You can ask for refills — just say <span class="hangul-cell">리필 해주세요!</span> (ri-pil hae-ju-se-yo!) It's completely normal!</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card" style="border-top:3px solid #1A4A8A;">
        <div class="card-title">🍽️ 함께 먹어요 — Eat Together!</div>
        <p>Korean food is communal. Dishes go in the CENTER of the table, and everyone shares. Eating alone (혼밥 · hon-bap) is trendy now, but sharing is the tradition!</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card" style="border-top:3px solid #27ae60;">
        <div class="card-title">🥃 소주 문화 — Soju Culture</div>
        <p>Never pour your own soju! Always pour for others, and let others pour for you. Hold your glass with <strong>two hands</strong> when receiving — it shows respect.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card" style="border-top:3px solid #d4850a;">
        <div class="card-title">🌶️ 얼마나 매워요? — How spicy?</div>
        <p>Tell the staff: <span class="hangul-cell">안 맵게 해주세요</span> (an maep-ge hae-ju-se-yo) — Please make it not spicy. Or: <span class="hangul-cell">아주 맵게!</span> (a-ju maep-ge!) — Very spicy!</p>
      </div>
    </div>
