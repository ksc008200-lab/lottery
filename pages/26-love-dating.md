    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">💕 Korean Dating Culture · 한국 연애 문화</h3>
    <div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:16px; margin:16px 0;">
      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #C0392B; padding:18px;">
        <div style="font-size:32px; margin-bottom:8px;">📅 100일 기념일</div>
        <div class="hangul-cell" style="font-size:16px; font-weight:700;">백일 기념일</div>
        <div style="font-size:12px; color:#666; margin:4px 0;">baek-il gi-nyeom-il</div>
        <p style="margin-top:8px; font-size:13px;">Koreans celebrate the <strong>100th day</strong> of dating — not just 1 year! Couples exchange gifts, eat out, and take photos. It's a milestone!</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #e91e8c; padding:18px;">
        <div style="font-size:32px; margin-bottom:8px;">👕 커플룩</div>
        <div class="hangul-cell" style="font-size:16px; font-weight:700;">커플룩 / 커플티</div>
        <div style="font-size:12px; color:#666; margin:4px 0;">keo-peul-luk / keo-peul-ti</div>
        <p style="margin-top:8px; font-size:13px;">Couples wear <strong>matching outfits</strong>! Matching T-shirts, sneakers, rings — it's super popular and considered adorable in Korea! 👫</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #7d3c98; padding:18px;">
        <div style="font-size:32px; margin-bottom:8px;">🍫 밸런타인데이</div>
        <div class="hangul-cell" style="font-size:16px; font-weight:700;">화이트데이</div>
        <div style="font-size:12px; color:#666; margin:4px 0;">hwa-i-teu-de-i · March 14th</div>
        <p style="margin-top:8px; font-size:13px;">Feb 14: Girls give chocolate to boys. <strong>March 14 (White Day)</strong>: Boys return the favor with candy! And April 14 (Black Day): Singles eat black noodles (짜장면) together! 🖤</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card" style="border-top:4px solid #d4850a; padding:18px;">
        <div style="font-size:32px; margin-bottom:8px;">📱 카톡</div>
        <div class="hangul-cell" style="font-size:16px; font-weight:700;">카카오톡 (카톡)</div>
        <div style="font-size:12px; color:#666; margin:4px 0;">ka-ka-o-tok (ka-tok)</div>
        <p style="margin-top:8px; font-size:13px;">KakaoTalk is THE messaging app in Korea. Couples chat on 카톡 constantly! Not replying quickly = danger zone. "읽씹" (read but no reply) = very bad sign! 😱</p>

    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">💬 The Confession Scene · 고백 장면</h3>
    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#fff0f5; border-radius:12px; padding:22px; margin:14px 0; border:1px solid #f8c8d8;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:12px; color:#C0392B;">🌹 고백 (Confession) · go-baek</div>
      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">저… 할 말이 있어요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(jeo... hal mal-i iss-eo-yo.) — Um... I have something to say.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">뭐예요? 갑자기 왜 이렇게 떨려요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(mwo-ye-yo? gap-ja-gi wae i-reo-ke tteol-lyeo-yo?) — What is it? Why am I suddenly nervous?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">사실… 많이 좋아해요. 처음 봤을 때부터요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(sa-sil... man-i jo-a-hae-yo. cheo-eum bwass-eul ttae-bu-teo-yo.) — Actually... I really like you. Since the first time I saw you.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">…저도요. 설레었어요. 우리 사귀어요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(...jeo-do-yo. seol-le-eoss-eo-yo. u-ri sa-gwi-eo-yo.) — ...Me too. My heart was fluttering. Let's date.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">진짜요?! 행복해요! 😍</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(jin-jja-yo?! haeng-bok-hae-yo!) — Really?! I'm so happy! 😍</span></p>
