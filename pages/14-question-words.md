    <!-- WHO 누가 -->
    <h3>1 · 누가 / 누구 — WHO · 누구</h3>
    <div class="card" style="background:#fff0f0; border-left:4px solid #C0392B; margin:12px 0;">
      <div class="card-title">📌 누가 vs 누구</div>
      <p><span class="hangul-cell">누가</span> (nu-ga) = Who? as the <strong>subject</strong> (who did the action?)<br>
      <span class="hangul-cell">누구</span> (nu-gu) = Who? in other positions (are you talking about who?)</p>
    </div>
    <table>
      <thead><tr><th>한글</th><th>발음</th><th>English</th></tr></thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">누가 왔어요?</td><td>nu-ga wass-eo-yo?</td><td>Who came?</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">이게 누구 거예요?</td><td>i-ge nu-gu geo-ye-yo?</td><td>Whose is this?</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">저 사람이 누구예요?</td><td>jeo sa-ram-i nu-gu-ye-yo?</td><td>Who is that person?</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">누구랑 왔어요?</td><td>nu-gu-rang wass-eo-yo?</td><td>Who did you come with?</td></tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>

    <!-- HOW 어떻게 -->
    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">6 · 어떻게 — HOW + 어때요 — HOW IS IT?</h3>
    <div class="card" style="background:#f0f7ff; border-left:4px solid #1A4A8A; margin:12px 0;">
      <div class="card-title">📌 어떻게 vs 어때요</div>
      <p><span class="hangul-cell">어떻게</span> (eo-tteo-ke) = <strong>How</strong> (method / manner) — "How do you do X?"<br>
      <span class="hangul-cell">어때요?</span> (eo-ttae-yo?) = <strong>How is it?</strong> — "What do you think? Is it okay?"</p>
    </div>
    <table>
      <thead><tr><th>한글</th><th>발음</th><th>English</th></tr></thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">어떻게 해요?</td><td>eo-tteo-ke hae-yo?</td><td>How do you do it?</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">이름이 어떻게 돼요?</td><td>i-reum-i eo-tteo-ke dwae-yo?</td><td>What is your name? (lit: how does your name go?)</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">여기 어떻게 가요?</td><td>yeo-gi eo-tteo-ke ga-yo?</td><td>How do I get here?</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">한국 음식 어때요?</td><td>han-guk eum-sik eo-ttae-yo?</td><td>How is Korean food? (Do you like it?)</td></tr>
        <tr><td class="hangul-cell">오늘 기분 어때요?</td><td>o-neul gi-bun eo-ttae-yo?</td><td>How do you feel today?</td></tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>

    <h3 style="margin-top:28px;">7 · 얼마 / 몇 — HOW MUCH / HOW MANY</h3>
    <div class="card" style="background:#fff8e7; border-left:4px solid #E8C84A; margin:12px 0;">
      <div class="card-title">📌 얼마 vs 몇</div>
      <p><span class="hangul-cell">얼마</span> (eol-ma) = How much? → <strong>price / amount</strong><br>
      <span class="hangul-cell">몇</span> (myeot) = How many? → <strong>countable number</strong></p>
    </div>

    <h3 style="margin-top:32px;">💬 Real Life Dialogs · 실생활 대화</h3>

    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:20px; margin:12px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:10px; color:#1A4A8A;">🛍️ Shopping · 쇼핑</div>
      <p><strong>Customer:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">이거 얼마예요?</span> (i-geo eol-ma-ye-yo?) — How much is this?</p>
      <p><strong>Staff:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">오만 원이에요.</span> (o-man won-i-e-yo.) — It's 50,000 won.</p>
      <p><strong>Customer:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">왜 이렇게 비싸요?</span> (wae i-reo-ke bi-ssa-yo?) — Why is it so expensive?</p>
      <p><strong>Staff:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">수제품이에요!</span> (su-je-pum-i-e-yo!) — It's handmade!</p>
    </div>

    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:20px; margin:12px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:10px; color:#1A4A8A;">🤝 Getting to Know Someone · 처음 만났을 때</div>
      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">어디서 왔어요?</span> (eo-di-seo wass-eo-yo?) — Where are you from?</p>
      <p><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">미국에서 왔어요. 어떻게 한국어를 배웠어요?</span> (mi-gu-ge-seo wass-eo-yo. eo-tteo-ke han-gu-geo-reul bae-woss-eo-yo?) — I'm from America. How did you learn Korean?</p>
      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">유튜브랑 앱으로 배웠어요!</span> (yu-tyu-beu-rang aep-eu-ro bae-woss-eo-yo!) — I learned via YouTube and apps!</p>
      <p><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">왜 한국어를 배워요?</span> (wae han-gu-geo-reul bae-wo-yo?) — Why are you learning Korean?</p>
      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">K-드라마가 너무 재미있어요! 😄</span> (K-deu-ra-ma-ga neo-mu jae-mi-iss-eo-yo!) — K-dramas are so fun!</p>
    </div>

    <div class="dialog-box" style="background:#f9f9f9; border-radius:12px; padding:20px; margin:12px 0; border:1px solid #e0ddd5;">
      <div style="font-weight:700; margin-bottom:10px; color:#1A4A8A;">🏥 At the Doctor · 병원에서</div>
      <p><strong>Doctor:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">어디가 아파요?</span> (eo-di-ga a-pa-yo?) — Where does it hurt?</p>
      <p><strong>Patient:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">머리가 아파요.</span> (meo-ri-ga a-pa-yo.) — My head hurts.</p>
      <p><strong>Doctor:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">언제부터 아팠어요?</span> (eon-je-bu-teo a-pass-eo-yo?) — Since when has it been hurting?</p>
      <p><strong>Patient:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">어제부터요. 얼마나 심각해요?</span> (eo-je-bu-teo-yo. eol-ma-na sim-ga-kae-yo?) — Since yesterday. How serious is it?</p>
      <p><strong>Doctor:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">괜찮아요. 약 드세요.</span> (gwaen-chan-a-yo. yak deu-se-yo.) — It's fine. Please take medicine.</p>
    </div>
