    <div class="card" style="background:#fff0f0; border-left:4px solid #C0392B; margin:20px 0;">
      <div class="card-title">🏢 Korean Workplace Culture · 한국 직장 문화</div>
      <div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:12px; margin-top:10px;">
        <div>
          <p><strong>👔 직급 (jik-geup)</strong> — job title/rank matters deeply. Always address senior colleagues with their title: 팀장님, 부장님, 대리님</p>
          <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>🍻 회식 (hoe-sik)</strong> — mandatory team dinner/drinking after work. Very important for team bonding — hard to skip!</p>
        </div>
        <div>
          <p><strong>⏰ 야근 (ya-geun)</strong> — working late is common and often expected. Leaving on time can raise eyebrows!</p>
          <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>☕ 점심 (jeom-sim)</strong> — lunch together as a team. Koreans rarely eat alone at the office. Food = bonding!</p>

      <div class="card-title">📚 학원 (Hagwon) — Korea's Private Academy System</div>
      <p>After regular school, most Korean students go to 학원 (private academies) for extra study — English, math, music, sports... Korean students often study until 10–11pm! This intense education culture is called <strong>교육열 (gyo-yung-yeol)</strong> — "education fever."</p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><span class="hangul-cell">학원 다녀요?</span> (hak-won da-nyeo-yo?) — Do you go to a hagwon?<br>
      <span class="hangul-cell">수능 준비 중이에요.</span> (su-neung jun-bi jung-i-e-yo.) — I'm preparing for the CSAT (college entrance exam).</p>

      <p><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">요즘 어떻게 지내요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(yo-jeum eo-tteo-ke ji-nae-yo?) — How have you been lately?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">바빠요! 요즘 야근이 너무 많아요. 거의 매일 밤 10시에 퇴근해요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(ba-ppa-yo! yo-jeum ya-geun-i neo-mu man-a-yo. geo-ui mae-il bam sip-si-e toe-geun-hae-yo.) — I'm busy! I've been working overtime a lot lately. I clock out at 10pm almost every night.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">헐, 힘들겠다. 이직 생각 없어요?</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(heol, him-deul-gess-da. i-jik saeng-gak eobs-eo-yo?) — Wow, that must be tough. Are you thinking about changing jobs?</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>B:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">있어요! 요즘 이직 준비하고 있어요. 포트폴리오 만들고 있어요.</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(iss-eo-yo! yo-jeum i-jik jun-bi-ha-go iss-eo-yo. po-teu-pol-li-o man-deul-go iss-eo-yo.) — I am! I'm preparing to change jobs now. I'm building a portfolio.</span></p>
      <p style="margin-top:8px;"><strong>A:</strong> <span class="hangul-cell">파이팅! 잘 될 거예요!</span><br>
      <span style="font-size:13px; color:#666;">(pa-i-ting! jal doel geo-ye-yo!) — Fighting! It'll work out!</span></p>
